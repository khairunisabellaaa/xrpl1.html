Prada - Bicester Village

Discover Prada, Church's, Miu Miu, & Car Shoe.

Focusing on rare and valuable materials that are long lasting and durable, Prada encapsulates sophisticated manufacturing techniques. Prada has become a well known household name, loved by customers from across the globe.

Visit the Prada store at Bicester Village and discover ready to wear handbags, purses, footwear, accessories, eyewear, fragrances and leather goods.

Prada Virtual Shopping

Prada is delighted to offer a Virtual Shopping service, allowing you to view and shop the Village collections at home. Your purchases will be made securely and delivered directly to your home.

Contact the boutique to arrange your consultation. This service is available Monday to Saturday from 9.00 to 21.00 and Sunday 12.00 to 18.00.
